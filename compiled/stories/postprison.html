Back To Work

by SCOTT THOMAS

Photographs by SCOTT THOMAS

Donald Gatewood, shift supervisor, and Keith Walker, Neighborhood Cleanup Project member, get ready for a shift cleaning up the vacant alleys on Chicago's South Side Thursday.

Keith Walker starts on Monday. Just like the first day of any job, it's a big deal. But it's especially true for Keith, who's most had temporary work since his arrest for driving a stolen vehicle in 1993.
